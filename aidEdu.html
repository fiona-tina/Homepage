Aid Education * (portfolio)

01.19.2018 ~ 01.31.2018

During winter vacation of 2018, I applied for aid education program of CS department and was selected as a member of aid education team. The whole team has to fulfill the schedule of one-week-long program for 7th & 8th year junior high school students in Baisha Middle School in Hainan province in China.

My plan was to design a practical oral English course to help those teenagers forming a better habit of learning English. My course covered listening to English songs and watching some videos with theme related question to test their understanding, Phonogram to help them learning better pronunciation and daily casual conversation topics to help them communicating with real foreigners.
